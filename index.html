About Us

Your books, done properly — without the big firm price tag.

Ledgerly is an ICB Level 3 certified solo bookkeeping practice working exclusively with sole traders and small Irish limited companies. Whether you're a tradesperson invoicing clients, a freelancer juggling multiple contracts, or a director of a small Irish Ltd managing payroll and CRO returns — Ledgerly is built for you.

Legal & Compliance

Professional, compliant and fully insured.

Ledgerly operates in accordance with the Institute of Certified Bookkeepers (ICB) code of professional conduct. We comply with Irish anti-money laundering legislation under the Criminal Justice (Money Laundering and Terrorist Financing) Acts 2010–2021, and all client data is held securely under GDPR and the Data Protection Acts 1988–2018.

All Revenue-facing submissions — VAT3, PAYE, RCT — are made via ROS (Revenue Online Service). Records are retained for a minimum of six years in line with Revenue requirements. A signed letter of engagement is issued before any work begins, so you always know exactly what's covered and what it costs.
